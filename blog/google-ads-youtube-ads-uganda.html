How Uganda Businesses Get Clients with Google Ads + YouTube Ads

Published by Asiimwe Lucky

If you want consistent clients, run ads to a focused landing page instead of a generic homepage. Start with one clear offer, one audience, and one call-to-action.

For Google Search Ads, target buyer-intent keywords such as "web designer Uganda", "app developer Kampala", and "Google Ads expert Uganda". Use location targeting and ad extensions.

For YouTube Ads, create short video ads with a single message and direct CTA to WhatsApp or booking. Retarget users who visited your pages but did not contact you.

Always track leads weekly and optimize based on cost per lead, not only clicks. Winning campaigns are improved consistently, not launched once and forgotten.
