School Website Marketing in Uganda: Admissions Growth Guide

Published by Asiimwe Lucky

Parents first check online trust signals before visiting a school. A strong website should show fees guidance, admissions process, school results, campus photos, and direct contacts.

Use local SEO phrases like "best school in [district]" and "boarding school in [location]". Publish events, achievements, and student activities regularly.

Add WhatsApp inquiry buttons to admissions pages and track which pages drive the most questions. Respond fast and use a simple admission info template.

With consistent updates and structured content, a school website becomes a reliable admissions funnel and trust platform.
